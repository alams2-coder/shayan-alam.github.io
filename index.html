About Me

A passionate biomedical engineering student with a drive to create innovative healthcare solutions and make a meaningful impact in the medical technology industry.

My Journey

Currently in my sophomore year of Biomedical Engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, I've discovered my passion for merging engineering principles with healthcare innovation. My journey began with curiosity about how technology can improve human health and has evolved into developing solutions that enhance diagnosis, treatment, and patient outcomes.

From leading the Biomedical Club in high school to contributing to the DEBUT competition team at RPI, I've consistently sought opportunities to apply engineering concepts to real-world medical challenges. My experience spans from teaching coding to kids to troubleshooting complex biomedical systems.

When I'm not studying or working on projects, you can find me participating in engineering societies, mentoring younger students, or exploring the latest innovations in biomedical technology.
